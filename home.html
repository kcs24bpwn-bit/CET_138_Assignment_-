About Nepal

Nepal is a beautiful country nestled in the Himalayas between India and China. Known for its stunning mountain landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality, Nepal attracts millions of visitors every year.

From the towering peaks of Mount Everest to the lush jungles of the Terai, Nepal offers incredible diversity in geography, climate, and culture. The country is home to ancient temples, vibrant festivals, and friendly people who welcome tourists with open arms.

Why Choose Nepal?

  • Home to 8 of the world's 14 highest mountains including Mt. Everest
  • Rich cultural heritage with UNESCO World Heritage Sites
  • Amazing trekking routes and adventure sports
  • Diverse wildlife including Bengal tigers and one-horned rhinos
  • Affordable travel destination with warm hospitality
  • Spiritual experience with ancient monasteries and temples
